Must-See Destinations in Pokhara

Discover the beautiful places in Pokhara and explore the activities they offer. From serene lakes to adventurous paragliding spots, there's something for everyone!

Phewa Lake

Phewa Lake is the second largest lake in Nepal, known for its scenic beauty and the Tal Barahi Temple located on an island in the lake.
